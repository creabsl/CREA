## 1. Introduction

Central Railway Engineers Association (CREA) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information of visitors and members using this website and our payment systems. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (including any associated applications), interact with us, and use our services including online donations and membership payments.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information You Provide

We may collect the following personal information when you:
- Create a membership account
- Make a donation
- Register for events
- Subscribe to newsletters
- Contact us for support
- Participate in forums or discussions

**Types of Personal Information:**
- **Name:** Full legal name or preferred name
- **Contact Information:** Email address, phone number, postal address
- **Membership Details:** Designation, division, department, unit
- **Professional Information:** Organization, role, experience level
- **Payment Information:** Transaction references only (we do NOT store card/bank/UPI details)
- **Account Credentials:** Username, password (hashed and encrypted)

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect:
- **Technical Data:** IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, time/date of access
- **Device Information:** Device type, unique identifiers, mobile network information
- **Cookies and Similar Technologies:** Session tokens, user preferences, login information
- **Analytics Data:** How you interact with our website, pages visited, time spent, search queries

### 2.3 Third-Party Information

- **Payment Gateway:** Razorpay provides transaction metadata (payment status, method, timestamp)
- **Email Service:** Gmail SMTP logs delivery status and timestamps
- **Social Media:** If you link your social accounts, we receive profile information

### 2.4 No Automatic Collection of Sensitive Data

We **DO NOT** collect or store:
- Credit card details
- Bank account information
- UPI IDs (except for reconciliation purposes)
- Sensitive personal information (SSN, government ID numbers)
- Biometric data
- Health or genetic information

## 8. Data Retention

### 8.1 Retention Schedule

We retain information for:

**Membership Records:**
- Active members: Retained during membership + 1 year after expiration
- Inactive members: 3 years for audit trail
- Purpose: Renewal offers, historical records, legal compliance

**Payment Records:**
- Active donations: 5 years (financial regulation compliance)
- Payment receipts: 7 years (tax compliance)
- Transaction references: 7 years (audit trail)

**Communication Records:**
- Emails: 2 years (support history)
- Event data: 3 years (participant history)

**Website Usage Data:**
- Analytics: 26 months (Google Analytics standard)
- Logs: 90 days (security monitoring)

**Sensitive Personal Information:**
- Deleted upon request (if legally permissible)
- Anonymized after retention period
- Securely destroyed when no longer needed

### 8.2 Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal information, except:
- When required by law (tax records, court orders)
- When necessary for ongoing disputes
- When needed for legitimate business purposes
- Aggregated/anonymized data (cannot be deleted)
